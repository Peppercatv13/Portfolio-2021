About Myself

"Proving development and design are ubiquitous."

I was born in Scottsdale, AZ and raised in Albuquerque, NM (and by an artist); art and aesthetic is deeply ingrained in who I am. An eye for the obscure and a passion for the palatable, I am enthusiastic to further my endeavor as a Web Designer to not only make beautiful, functional and practical pieces of art and software; but make it accessible as an art and design medium. I studied Music performance (flute) at Morehead State University I thrive under pressure, am an accute problem solver and have a voracious learning curve. I have never felt more at home in the midst of the unknown than I am as a fledgling web-developer.
